Cooking : Meat

Kashmir is well know for various types, tastes of meat. A major amount of Kashmir's residents are muslims. This also means that there is a majority of meat in their diets. Kashmiris always marinate and season the meat(depending on the cut and slice of the meat), so I went ahead and categorized those processes.

Process Type of change
Seasoning Physical Change (Evaporation of water) induces a Chemical Change (Mixing of chemical components present in spices with food, thus inducing flavour.)
Marination Chemical Change(breaking down of fibres ie. protein fold change) induces Physical Change(softness)

In seasoning evaporation of water causes the spices to diffuse with the meat, and change of state of Water (H2O) is considered as a physical change. This induces a chemical change wherein chemical bonds of the spices and the meat change, and the compounds mix.

Carpets and Shawls

Kashmir is also known for Pashmina Shawls, Carpets. In fact, they are very large, but still, mostly hand woven in Kashmir. There is an interesting story to this, the pashmina hair is obtained through finely spinning Cashmere from the Changthangi goat. The quality depends on the number of knots. There's science behind this too. More knots leads to more air being trapped, thus ensuring more insulation.

Process Type of change
Growth of Cashmere goat Chemical Change : Usage of energy and nutrients.
Obtaining of Cashmere Wool Physical Change : Combing from undercoat of Cashmere Goat.
Processing of Cashmere to Pashmina Chemical Change : Using water, acids, bleach, dyes and processing Cashmere.
Spinning and Weaving Pashmina Physical Change : Spinning the fibres together(Spinning), Interlocking spun yarn(Weaving).

Apples

Indian Apples are known for their crunchy, crispy, sweet nature. Apples are grown on a large scale in northern states of India. In Kashmir, it is a common practice to mix ripe apples with raw apples in baskets and stacking them. Orchards do so, as apples release a special hormone/gas called ethylene. It is a vital hormone in the ripening of fruits. So any fruit set near ripening apples will ripen faster.

Process Type of change
Ripening of apples Chemical change : usage of energy, secretion of hormones.
Ripening of other fruits, using apples. Chemical change : usage of energy, secretion of hormones.

Papier-mâché

Kashmiri papier-mâché is a handicraft of Kashmir that was brought from Persia in the 14th century to medieval India. It is based on paper pulp, and is a beautiful artifact, in the form of vases or bowls, cups, boxes, trays, bases of lamps, other objects. These are made in homes, and workshop in parts of the Kashmir Valley, and are marketed primarily within India, although there is a significant international market. It has a GI tag.

Process Type of change
Preparing the paper pulp. Chemical Change : Paper pulp, is produced through soaking paper in water with some acids, thereby loosening fibres.
Making the model Physical Change : Mixing with adhesive solution and moulding.

Kashmiri Willow Bats

This is not a well known fact to many. English Cricket Bats cost a lot and are not affordable and suitable to all players, especially budding ones. Here, Kashmiri Willow Bats offer a good alternative to English Bats, providing a lighter, easy to strike body at a relatively lower price, with a commendable performance.

Process Type of change
Pounding and moulding wood Physical Change : cutting, beating and shaping of wood.
